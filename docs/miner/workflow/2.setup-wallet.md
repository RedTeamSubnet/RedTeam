# 2. Setup Your Bittensor Wallet

You need to set up your Bittensor wallet to participate in the RedTeam Subnet challenges. This involves creating a new wallet, funding it with TAO tokens, and registering it on the RedTeam Subnet 61.

## 2.0. Prerequisites

- [**1. Prepare Environment**](./1.prepare-environment.md) for your workspace.

## 2.1. Setup your wallet

- [**2.1.1. Create Your Wallet**](../../manuals/bittensor/wallet/create.md): Create a new Bittensor wallet
- [**2.1.2. Fund Your Wallet**](../../manuals/bittensor/wallet/fund.md): Fund your wallet with TAO tokens
- [**2.1.3. Register Your Wallet on Subnet**](../../manuals/bittensor/wallet/register.md): Register your wallet on the RedTeam Subnet 61
